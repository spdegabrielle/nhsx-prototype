We’re here to improve patient care and health outcomes

NHSX is a new joint unit that will drive digital transformation in health and social care.

Background

The digital transformation of the NHS is the most important change we will make to healthcare for the next decade. But the responsibility for it is currently split between numerous organisations and teams.

We need a transformation in the way that technology is led in the NHS. NHSX brings together a new joint unit in the Department of Health and Social Care and NHS England to drive digital transformation and lead policy, implementation and change.

Find out more about our background

“One unit to set national policy, remove red tape and create a culture of innovation to allow the best innovations to flourish.”

Matt Hancock, Health Secretary

What we do

We are here to:

  • use technology to speed up the transformation of the NHS and social care - for patients and staff
  • use the opportunities of digital technology to meet growing demand in the NHS
  • simplify decision-making so that digital programmes advance rapidly
  • focus on the needs of the public and of staff
  • make it easier for NHS staff to focus on their patients
  • give people easy access to great digital services and their data to help them stay well and manage their own care
  • make sure that patients’ data is kept secure and used well for their benefit
  • make the NHS is the best place for health and care tech entrepreneurs

Find out more about what we do

How we work

  • We listen, try to understand and communicate clearly.
  • We encourage everyone to contribute their skills, knowledge and experience. Everyone’s voice is equal and respected.
  • We are user-centred and we work openly and with a wide range of partners because that will deliver better outcomes.
  • We value ambition, are open to learning and new ideas, and support people to innovate.
  • We work collaboratively, beyond job roles and across teams.
  • We recognise and celebrate success and take ownership of problems and solutions.
